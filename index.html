About Holi

The Festival of Colors

Holi is an ancient Hindu festival that celebrates the triumph of good over evil and the arrival of spring. Known as the "Festival of Colors" or the "Festival of Love," it signifies the end of winter and the abundance of the upcoming spring harvest season.

During Holi, people gather to throw colored powders and water at each other, breaking down social barriers and bringing people together in joyful celebration. Traditional sweets are shared, and communities come together to dance, sing, and rejoice.
